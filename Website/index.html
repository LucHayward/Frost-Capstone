Frost

Keegan White (WHTKEE004) | Jesse Mark (MRKJS003) | Luc Hayward (HYWLUC001)

summary

Frost is an endless arena style game in which the player (or players in split-screen co-op), must fight against the hordes of vicious Mutants, Zombies and Vampires in the amphitheatre of the Frostlands.

Gameplay

Player
The player (or players) take on the role of a wizard to defeat the hordes of monsters inhabiting the battleground.

Frost Essence
Downed enemies may drop frost essence allowing the wizards to make use of their abilities and turn the tide of battle on their foes.

Abilities
Players have 3 abilities powered by the frost essence they collect.
1) A Healing spell, useful for staying alive in combat
2) A Damage spell, amplify your power to dispatch you foes
3) A Speed spell, perfect for when you're about to be overwhelmed

Enemies
Zombies: The weakest of all the enemies you may face, be careful not to underestimate them as they have a habit of working together to overrun even the most powerful of wizards.
Witches: These long-dead sorcerors attack with a deadly flurry of long range magical attacks. Don't lose track of them during battle or you may quickly find the tides turning against you.
Mutant: Little is known of the mutants or how they came to grow so large in such a barren land. What they lack in speed they more than make up for through sheer strength and stamina making them your most formiddable opponent when they appear.

Controls

Mouse and Keyboard:
WASD: Movement
Mouse: Camera Control
Left Click: Primary Attack
Right Click: Melee Attack
Space: Stun Ability
Shift: Speed Boost
Q: Damage Boost
E: Heal Ability
F: Interact/Open

Controller:
4Left Analogue Stick: Movement
14Right Analogue Stick: Camera Control
8Right Trigger: Primary Attack
1Left Trigger: Melee Attack
9Right Bumper: Stun Ability
2Left bumper: Speed Boost
12Square: Damage Boost
13X: Heal Ability
12Circle: Interact/Open
